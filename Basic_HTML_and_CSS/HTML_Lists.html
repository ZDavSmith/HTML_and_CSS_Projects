How to Rocket League Kickoff:

  1. Move Joystick Forward Towards Ball
  2. Hold Right Trigger to Drive
  3. Hold B to Boost
  4. Let Go of B After Half a Second
  5. Double Tap A to Front Flip
  6. Hold B After Car's Rotation is Facing Forward Again
  7. Either Drive Straight Into the Ball or Double Tap A to Front Flip
